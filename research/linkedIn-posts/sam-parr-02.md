**My Note**: This is exactly like the "hiring city switch". I see founders do this all the time: When remote work hype hit, they immediately hire devs overseas. 6 months later they come back to local devs. Why? Because hype says "cheap + lots of talent" but reality says "time zone issues + culture gap + communication mess". Hype makes you move. Reality makes you come back.
