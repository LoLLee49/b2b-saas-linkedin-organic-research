**Pattern**: Small habit → Personal upside → Organizational upside → Call for structural change
**Key Insight**: The advice isn’t:
Learn AI.
It’s:
Build with AI.
Consumption creates awareness.
Building creates capability.
The hidden belief:
The future advantage won’t come from knowing AI exists. It comes from developing instinct for what can be automated, accelerated, or reinvented.
Three hours/week isn’t about productivity.
It’s about creating a compounding learning loop.

**My Note**: This made me rethink learning.
Maybe the goal isn’t to master AI.
Maybe the goal is to become someone who builds before the playbook exists.
Summarize in one line
Treat AI like practice time, not reading time—small weekly experiments compound into career leverage.
**Repurpose Angle**: You don’t need a side project.
