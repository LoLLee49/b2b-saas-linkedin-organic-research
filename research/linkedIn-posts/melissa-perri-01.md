**Full Text (summary, not verbatim)**:
Melissa Perri explains that AI is lowering the cost of building to near zero, enabling teams to prototype and ship features in days instead of months. However, this acceleration creates a dangerous side effect: it becomes easier to build the wrong things at scale. Companies risk falling into a “build trap” where they mistake output velocity for actual product success. Without strong product fundamentals, shared understanding of customer problems, and alignment across teams, organizations simply produce more features that fail to deliver value. The post emphasizes that AI does not replace product thinking — it raises the importance of it. Teams must invest in clear problem definition, strategic alignment, and outcome-driven decision-making to avoid turning speed into chaos.
