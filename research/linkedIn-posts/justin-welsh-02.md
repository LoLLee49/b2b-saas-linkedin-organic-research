**Full Text**:
Everyone wants the result. 
Few people want the work.
When I started, I pictured the good part. 
The freedom, the income, the work that felt like mine. 
I didn't picture how long the unglamorous middle would last.
Years of writing things nobody read. 
Years where the numbers barely moved, and the only proof I had was that I hadn't quit yet.
Then somewhere around year four, something shifted. 
I stopped checking whether it was working and started enjoying the work itself.
