**Full Text**:
One of the biggest mistakes founders make when pitching investors. 

They speak badly about incumbents/competition. DON’T. 

They have done something right to get to where they are. 

State to investors: 

1. Where they have done well?

2. That you have analysed their successes as well as their failures. 

3. State a plan for how you build upon their successes and learn from failures. 

The best founders are students of history.
